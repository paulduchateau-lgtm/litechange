Gradient Rule Notice

Design system constraint
Gradient backgrounds are reserved for the hero section only.
Dashboard organisms, content sections, card grids, and all other components use flat surface tokens (--liteops-surface-primary, --liteops-surface-secondary, etc.).

Available hero gradients:
• --liteops-gradient-hero-green-olive -- primary dark hero (green to olive 135deg)
• --liteops-gradient-hero-green-black -- deeper dark hero (green to black 135deg)
• --liteops-gradient-hero-subtle -- light hero (subtle green fade 180deg)

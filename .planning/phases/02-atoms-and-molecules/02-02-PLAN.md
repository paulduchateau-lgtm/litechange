- Focus: `outline: 2px solid var(--liteops-border-focus); outline-offset: 2px` on the track when input is `:focus-visible`
- Disabled: `opacity: 0.5; cursor: not-allowed` on wrapper
- Showcase: on state, off state, disabled on, disabled off, with label, dark theme panel
- Accessibility: label associated via `<label>` wrapping or `for` attribute
